My USA West Coast Holiday 2019

This summer I had the holiday of a life time with my family. We visited the west coast of America and took in as many sights as possible in 2 weeks. The map below shows the route we took on a 3 day camping trip to take in most of the sights. We visited the following National Parks :

  • Zion National Park
  • Bryce Canyon
  • Antelope Canyon
  • Monument Valley
  • Grand Canyon
  • Yosemite National Parks

On our journey we visited 4 states and the Navajo Indian reservation which sits between Arizona and Utah

  2. Arizona
